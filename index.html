About Us

DigiMed is a team of dedicated, hardworking and like minded people formed to create a Niche in Healthcare Information Technology .

DigiMed is created to move the healthcare-provider sector towards high quality, evidence driven, cost effective and patient centric service delivery through professionalized systems, processes and capabilities...

Our Mission

To provide innovative healthcare IT solutions in a service focused environment which will improve delivery, coordination & efficiencies thereby enhancing the quality of care and increase value for healthcare organizations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a regional leader in Health IT by leveraging our information, software and service solutions to connect all key stakeholders and realize the full potential in the Healthcare ecosystem.

Our Value Proposition

  • Team of Healthcare Functional and Technical Experts combined to create a dedicated Healthcare Service organization
  • Core team built on deep regional experience in Healthcare
  • Proven track record
  • Unique innovation and localisations
  • Distinctive approach and methodology
